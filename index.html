About Ribbons.Guide

Ribbons.Guide is an open-source progressive web app that allows you to keep track of your Pokémon and their Ribbons! Ribbons.Guide is hosted via GitHub Pages. It was developed as a love letter to the helpful and welcoming r/PokémonRibbons community.

The images used in Ribbons.Guide were provided by PokéJungle, Pokémon Database, PokéSprite, Bulbagarden Archives, Pokémon GO Assets, and Material Symbols. Ribbons.Guide also makes use of jQuery, Bootstrap, Select2, Sortable, Workbox, and Google Fonts. This project is tested with BrowserStack.
